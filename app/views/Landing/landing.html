Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Corn Cradle

How does the corn auction process work?

Our auction process is simple and transparent. Farmers list their corn with detailed specifications, buyers place bids, and the highest bidder wins. All transactions are secured through our platform, and we provide real-time updates throughout the auction process.

What are the fees for using Corn Cradle?

We charge a small commission fee on successful transactions. The exact fee structure varies based on the transaction amount and type. There are no hidden charges, and all fees are clearly displayed before you participate in any auction.

How do I ensure the quality of the corn I'm buying?

All corn listed on our platform undergoes a quality verification process. We require detailed specifications, including moisture content, grade, and other quality parameters. Additionally, we have a rating system where buyers can review their purchases, helping maintain quality standards.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various secure payment methods including bank transfers, digital wallets, and other electronic payment systems. All transactions are protected by our secure payment gateway, ensuring safe and reliable transfers.

How do I get started as a farmer or buyer?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete the verification process, and you can start participating in auctions. Farmers can list their corn immediately, while buyers can browse and bid on available lots. Our support team is available to guide you through the process.
